When forces are coherence-structured (clustered motifs; sparse long-range couplings), the serial walker often reduces stiffness locally before corrections propagate globally; in homogeneous or strongly chaotic regimes, standard symplectic methods (e.g., leapfrog) may still be preferred for long-time Hamiltonian fidelity.
